Metal Solutions

Metal Solutions Ltd are predominantly Multi-Metal and Multi-System distributors to the traditional roofing and cladding industry. We are far more than distributors though and due to our many years of technical and contracting experience, we are able to offer specific advice on all Metals and Systems. Whichever Metal and System combination you may choose, we can provide a range of standard details for each option.

Metals

All Manufacturers will tell you that their particular metal is the best, however the best Metal is the one that satisfies the Client’s needs the most. Metal Solutions Ltd can offer impartial advice on all metals, whether it be Aluminium, Brass, Bronze, Copper, Stainless Steel, Steel, Zinc or other alloys.

We can assist you through the metal minefield and explain the nuances of each material, whether it be the colour choices, workability, weathering process, what to expect as the metal ages, ultimate life span etc, as well as the all-important eco credentials. Please visit our Metals page, to see these important differences and learn which one(s) will suit your project the best.

The Manufacturers that we work with are Aurubis copper, Falzonal aluminium, GreenCoat steel, Kalzip Falzinc aluminium, NedZink zinc, Prefa Prefalz aluminium, Roofinox stainless steel, Traditional Textures aluminium, Zintek and ZM Silesia zinc.

All metals are processed in-house from our large stocks of master coils, so all combinations of coil width and length, as well as sheets are available to order.

If you already know which Metal you require then please have a look at what we offer, we are still here to help. All our Metal and System are available for specification via the NBS (National Building Specification).

Systems

As with Metals, systems are a personal choice and we can advise on the many systems available, whether it be standing seam, Snaplock® seam, batten roll, shingle, shiplap panel, flatlock panel, reveal panel (also known as plank panel or interlocking panel), sinusoidal panel, perforated products, Topclip coping, rainwater goods or even project specific and bespoke solutions.

Products

Metal Solutions Ltd offer a complete range of ancillary products for example, support structures, underlays, clips, fixings, snow guards, seam clamps etc. We offer our own brands such as ISO-Mat Light® & ISO-Mat Pro® underlays, ISO-Mat Metal® structured underlay, Snaplock® standing seam, Thermoseam® warm roof clips and the GreenLine® rainwater system.
